What we're building

Most apps tell you what to do.
We tell you why.

The Seasonal House is a farming and homestead companion built around one simple idea: knowledge beats checklists. Every task comes with three layers — what to do, how to do it right, and why it matters right now.

Whether you're keeping two hives in your backyard or managing a 200-hive commercial operation across six yards, the app adapts to your scale, your experience, and your season.

✦
What
Add a pollen patty to Hive 2 before Friday's cold snap.
▸
How
Open the hive on a day above 50°F, center the patty above the cluster on top of the frames.
◎
Why
Early spring colonies need protein to raise brood before natural pollen arrives. A well-fed colony now means a strong forager force when nectar flow begins.

The philosophy

A knowledgeable neighbor,
not a manual

Seasonal first

You shouldn't need an app to make a good decision. The Seasonal House is designed to build your understanding over time — not keep you guessing.
